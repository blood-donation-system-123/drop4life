1. Check Eligibility

✔ Minimum age 18 ✔ Weight above 50 kg ✔ No major illness ✔ Normal blood pressure

2. Pre-Donation Test

A small drop of blood is taken to check your haemoglobin levels and ensure you are fit to donate.

3. Actual Donation Process

- You lie down comfortably - A sterile needle is used - Around 350–450 ml of blood is collected - Entire process takes 8–12 minutes

4. Post-Donation Care

- Rest for 10 minutes - Eat a snack / juice - Avoid heavy exercise for the day

5. Your Blood Saves Lives

Your single donation can help **3 different people** through components like plasma, platelets, and red cells.
